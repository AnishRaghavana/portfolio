Mahindra — Campaign Executions

01
The Mahindra Field Fest
A one-day country music festival in San Marcos, Texas — built around families, farmers, and the rural way of life, powered by Mahindra. Live tractor demos, on-site dealer connections, and a stage that put the brand at the center of the culture it was trying to own. Not an ad. An experience that converts fans into customers.
Projected 15,000–20,000 qualified attendees
02
Rodeo Circuit & Mechanical Tractor Bull
Trade show presence at the Austin Rodeo with one twist: a pivot-mounted tractor reimagined as a mechanical bull. Safe, memorable, and built to stop traffic. Mahindra shows up at the rodeo not as a sponsor, but as part of the show — turning a product demo into a cultural moment that rural audiences actually talk about.
Trade show + rodeo circuit activation
03
National Agriculture Day Influencer Drop
A timed influencer series launching on National Agriculture Day — short-form video showing real people prepping land, running projects, and showcasing Mahindra equipment doing the work. Product-for-content trade instead of cash, keeping the budget lean and the content authentic. Three-part cadence over six weeks to build sustained engagement.
Series 1: "Tested on Our Field"  ·  Series 2: "Tough Jobs Made Easy"  ·  Series 3: "Spring Strong"
