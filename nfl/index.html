Push Forward — Campaign Executions Two creative worlds. Four pillars. One campaign. Designed and art directed by Anish Raghavan.
Sonic — Physical & Social Pillars A custom flag football kit built around movement and identity. Sonic-checkered football, custom gloves, flag belts with a text-shattering color scheme and hand-drawn Sonic characters. Four collectible zone cards — one per pillar — that kids earn and trade.
Brick by Brick — Emotional & Intellectual Pillars Custom LEGO bricks color-coded to each pillar. A live interactive mosaic where parents and kids place bricks side by side — then watch the finished piece get hoisted onto a billboard. Plus Brick by Brick: a TV series mockup with a custom logo and hand-textured wallpaper, about what families build when they finally show up for each other.

Designed by Anish Raghavan
