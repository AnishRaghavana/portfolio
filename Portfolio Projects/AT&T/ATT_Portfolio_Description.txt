AT&T Digital Nomads — "Create Your Digital Destiny"

I helped build a full-funnel campaign for AT&T targeting Gen Z, repositioning the brand from a utility into a launchpad for digital nomadic freedom. I worked with a team of six to take the project from primary research — 133 survey respondents, focus groups, competitive analysis — to a finished 22-page plansbook with creative executions across every channel.

I dug into the data and helped surface the core insight: Gen Z doesn't want a phone plan, they want infrastructure for an unconventional life. That became "Create Your Digital Destiny" — a campaign built around 5G as the backbone of freedom, flexibility, and self-expression.

I contributed to social mockups for Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok; a humor-driven commercial storyboard; podcast and radio scripts; stadium activations with drone coupon drops and QR code ads; and an earned media strategy spanning Reddit AMAs, a mascot competition, and a community docuseries.

Research. Strategy. Creative. Execution. All of it, start to finish.
