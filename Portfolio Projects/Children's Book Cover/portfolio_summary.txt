Texas Recipe Book Cover — Medical City Children's Hospital (2022–23)

I designed the cover for the Texas Recipe Book 2022–23, a children's publication produced in partnership with Medical City Children's Hospital and the Texas Restaurant Foundation. I was given a brief that challenged me to merge two themes — fruits & vegetables and outer space — into a single, cohesive illustration for a young audience.

My concept centers on a carrot rocket ship blasting off through a vibrant purple sky, with leafy green clouds framing the launch. I incorporated a strawberry as the rocket's nose cone to reinforce the nutritional theme while adding a fun, unexpected detail that kids would delight in. I wanted healthy food to feel adventurous and exciting rather than ordinary.

I used bold linework, bright saturated colors, and a hand-lettered typographic style to keep the tone warm, energetic, and age-appropriate — while ensuring the composition reads clearly even at smaller print sizes.

This project allowed me to flex my skills in concept development, editorial illustration, children's design, and visual storytelling — all within a meaningful cause-driven context.
