ASRA came to me with a clear vision: build a brand that felt modern without losing its humanity. The brief called for a wellness app identity — something that would resonate with users seeking balance, movement, and vitality in their daily lives.

The challenge was striking the right tension between structure and softness. Too clinical, and it loses the warmth wellness demands. Too organic, and it loses the authority a digital product needs to earn trust.

The solution was built on flow.

Every element of the ASRA identity was designed around curves and continuous motion — a deliberate nod to the rhythms of the human body and the cyclical nature of wellbeing. The icon depicts a figure in full expression, arms open, grounded by a sweeping ribbon of color that moves like breath. Nothing is rigid. Nothing is accidental.

The color palette reinforces this duality: deep navy for authority and grounding, gold for energy and vitality, sky blue for clarity and calm. Together they hold the balance the client asked for — professional enough for an app store, alive enough to mean something.

Deliverables: Primary logo with horizontal lockup, white and dark color variations, stacked logo variations, brand stamp and icon mark, app icon, and a branded Zoom background.
