As Account Director for our capstone campaign with Artist Boat, I had
the privilege of leading the development of a comprehensive marketing
plansbook that brought our team’s strategy and creativity to life. Built
around a $100,000 integrated campaign, the plansbook outlines our
audience insights, competitive analysis, brand positioning, campaign
messaging, media strategy, and a full rollout plan designed to help the
client grow their impact.

I worked closely with a team of 20 members across Account, Media, and
Creative, helping guide collaboration and alignment so that every part
of the campaign connected back to a single vision. From shaping the
strategy to bringing together everyone’s work into the final plansbook,
my goal was to make sure the story of the campaign felt clear,
intentional, and meaningful.

What means the most to me from this experience is the people I had the
chance to work with. I am incredibly proud of my classmates and the
passion they brought to this project. Everyone showed up with ideas,
curiosity, and a genuine drive to create something that mattered.
Watching those ideas grow, challenge each other, and eventually come
together into one campaign was inspiring. Being surrounded by classmates
who care deeply about the craft of advertising pushed me to be a better
leader and collaborator. The final plansbook is not just a campaign. It
is a reflection of the energy, creativity, and heart that our entire
team poured into the work.
