I built a brand identity presentation for Fora, my debate social platform. Fora is built around the idea that debate is a sport — structured, competitive, and thrilling to watch. The logo itself embodies this: the podium icon doubles as the letter F, making the mark inseparable from the product's purpose.

The presentation walks through the brand story, logo anatomy, approved configurations, color palette, brand voice, and a real-world LinkedIn mockup — all designed on a true black canvas that lets the purple and white brand colors hit with maximum contrast. I iterated through several versions live, refining the deck down to a tight 8 slides that communicate the brand clearly and confidently.
