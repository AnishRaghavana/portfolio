TULU – Launch Automation System

At TULU, I built an automation system that simplified how new buildings
launched on the platform. Starting from a database of properties, I
created workflows that generated QR codes, built product listings, and
produced launch pages for each building. Everything fed into a central
sheet that organized the data and powered automated marketing collateral
like flyers, digital billboards, business cards, posters, and email
campaigns.

Working inside a startup changed how I approach problems. Things move
fast, resources are limited, and you have to figure things out as you
go. I learned to adapt quickly, build scrappy solutions, and keep
improving systems once they were live.

More than anything, this experience shaped how I think. I became
comfortable building without a perfect roadmap, solving problems
independently, and turning rough ideas into working systems. That
startup mindset and willingness to figure things out has become one of
the most valuable things I took from my time at TULU.
